What is Covid-19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus. The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing.

What to do to stay protected?

  • Everyone must wash their hands often, for at least 20 seconds with soap and water.
  • Do not touch your face or eyes!
  • Wipe down surfaces, especially steel and others that are frequently touched
  • Self-Quarantine and stay at least 6 feet away from a person
  • When outside try to wear cloth over your mouth(N-95 mask).
